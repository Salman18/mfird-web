Millat Foundation for Industry, Research and Development

MFIRD

Millat foundation for Industries' research and Development is an associate of MFERD, which is a non-profit organization for khidmat-e-khalq (Services to the creation) MFIRD is an endeavour to update and upgrade all sections of the Ummah with the latest developments in various industries/technologies.
It's our responsibility to keep abreast with the constant changes and advancements in our respective fields and disseminate this knowledge to others. With this belief and the sole purpose of pleasing Allah swt, MFIRD undertakes various initiatives such as
1. Conferences on latest industry trends
2. Career guidance and counseling for the working professionals
3. Job orientation for freshers
4. Internships and projects for students
Alhamdulillah, these initiatives have benefitted the Ummah in various colleges, startups, individuals.
Continuous effort with the blessings bestowed upon us and working together is the need of our times.
Let's join hands in this journey to bring out the best of this Ummah.
