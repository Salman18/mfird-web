Millat Foundation for Industry, Research and Development

MISSION

We aim to achieve our vision by creating a conductive platform that,

  • Promotes the culture of Up skilling to stay relevent in the industry
  • Motivates professionals to embark on an Enterpreneurial journey
  • Provides Career Guidance to budding professionals
  • Facilitates volunteering of time to equip needy school children with skills to become better individuals

VISION
To provide a common platform for Professionals across industries to share, network, co-ordinate and co-operate; help them achieve excellence in their respective fields and build a culture of giving back to society.
